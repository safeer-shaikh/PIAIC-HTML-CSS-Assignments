PIAIC

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (PIAIC) is a privately funded not-for-profit educational initiative started by President of pakistan, Dr Arif Alvi, and Zia Khan to help promote education, research and business opportunities in emerging tech fields like

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Atificial Intelligence
  • BlockChain Computing
  • Internet Of Things

Cloud Native Computing:

The Cloud Native Computing Program prepares the students to program in open source technologies like, linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, React Nativewhile introducing the students to development of Microservices and softwares development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence

The artificial Intelligence program of PIAIC takes the student through the foundation of the AI technology, via teaching programming in open source languages like, Python, Numpy, Pandas,TenserFlow 2.0, and Flaskand teaching deploying practical AI soluction and use cases and ending with real life examples of modern day AI in practice

BlockChain Computing

The main Emphasis of BlockChain program is to understand the blockchain like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabrics) and learn to program in language such as solidity, JavaScript, framworks like node.js. The program also introduce them to development of Distributed Apps,(DApps) framwordslike web3, and truffle and an in-depth understanding of blockchain-as-a-service (BaaS), and how to develop and offer an initial coin offer (ICO)of a crypto-token

Internet of Things

the Internet of things program introduce the students to open source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rush, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes, while making them efficient in developing Micro Services for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice Computing for device control
